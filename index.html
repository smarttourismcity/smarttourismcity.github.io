Parangtritis Beach

4.6 Review 8.822 Address

Enjoy the natural charm of Parangtritis Beach, an iconic destination in Yogyakarta that offers sepctacular views and an alluring atmosphere. With its distinctive volcanic black sand and rolling waves, Parangtritis is the perfect place to enjoy a stunning sunset. Explore the local myths and legends that add to the charm of this beach, and experience the wonders of nature blended with a rich culture.

Parangkusumo Beach

4.5 8 ratings form 0.31$-6.20$ destination

Parangkusumo Beach is known as a place full of mystical and spiritual nuances. Located not far from Parangtritis Beach, this beach is the location for various traditional rituals that are loaded with cultural values. Enjoy the beauty of nature while diving into the local wisdom that emanates from every corner of Parangkusumo Beach. Don't miss the opportunity to feel the sensation of walking on the soft sand and hear the soothing roar of the waves.

Depok Beach

5.0 15 ratings form 0.31$-6.20$ destination

Depok Beach is a paradise for marine culinary lovers. Here, you can enjoy a variety of delicious fresh seafood dishes, straight from the sea to your table. Take a stroll along the beach while enjoying the stunning ocean views, then stop by the stalls that offer a variety of processed fish, shrimp, crab and other seafood. Depok Beach is the perfect destination to experience culinary enjoyment and natural beauty at the same time.

Cemara Sewu Beach

Cemara Sewu Beach offers tranquility and natural beauty. With its lush rows of cypress trees and a cool atmosphere, this beach is an ideal place to relax and enjoy nature. Explore the beauty of this beach by walking among the cypress trees, or sit in their shade while enjoying the refreshing sea breeze.
