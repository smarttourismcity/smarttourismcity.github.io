How to find your Desired Place more quickly: Parangtritis Beach

posted by admin July 2024

A beautiful beach but worth being aware of because it has the potential to be dragged by very high currents. The existence of RIP Current, a seemingly calm seaside area, but a strong and high-speed ocean current. Beautiful and magical are two words that are suitable to describe Parangtritis Beach because it is a mainstay tourist attraction in Yogyakarta. This beach presents a view for tourists to watch the sunrise and sunset. The beach area also offers other activities such as walking along the beach by ATV and Jeep. Tourists are still urged by the community to continue to uphold and respect the customs that apply in the place.

Exploring the Unique Wonders of Gumuk Pasir Yogyakarta

posted by admin July 2024

Nestled in the heart of Yogyakarta, Indonesia, lies a hidden gem that offers a truly unique experience for tourists: the Gumuk Pasir. This extraordinary destination is a natural sand dune located in Parangtritis, Bantul, Yogyakarta, and is often referred to as the "Sahara of Java." The Gumuk Pasir is not only a geological wonder but also a hub for exciting outdoor activities and cultural experiences.

Exploring Parangtritis Geomaritime Science Park: A Journey into Yogyakarta's Maritime Heritage

posted by admin July 2024

The Parangtritis Geomaritime Science Park offers a unique and enriching experience for anyone interested in maritime history, science, and environmental conservation. Its engaging exhibits, educational programs, and stunning coastal location make it a standout attraction in Yogyakarta. Whether you're a local resident or a tourist, a visit to this Science Park promises to be both informative and inspiring, providing a deeper appreciation for Indonesia's maritime heritage.
