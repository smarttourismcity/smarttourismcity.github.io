Explore Amazing Place:

There are 14 Tourist Attractions on the coast of Parangtritis Beach, Depok, Bantul, Yogyakarta

  • Imagine a place where the sea whispers ancient tales, the sand cradles your feet with warmth, and every corner holds a secret waiting to be discovered. Welcome to Parangtritis Beach in Depok, Kretek, Bantul—a coastal paradise that offers not just a beach, but a myriad of experiences across its 14 unique attractions.

  • 1. The Grand Entrance: Parangtritis Beach

    Your adventure begins at the iconic Parangtritis Beach, where the vast expanse of golden sands meets the azure embrace of the Indian Ocean. Feel the breeze in your hair as you stroll along the shore, marvel at the majestic cliffs, and take in the panoramic views that stretch as far as the eye can see.

  • 2. The Mystical Parangkusumo Sand Dunes

    Just a short distance away, the Parangkusumo Sand Dunes await. These natural wonders resemble the rolling deserts of Arabia, offering a surreal landscape perfect for sandboarding. Glide down the sandy slopes, feel the thrill, and let your spirit soar.

  • 3. The Enchanting Depok Beach

    Venture further to Depok Beach, a tranquil haven where the rhythm of the waves is soothing, and the seafood is a culinary delight. Indulge in freshly caught fish and savor the local flavors while gazing at the horizon where the sky meets the sea.

  • 4. The Cultural Heartbeat: Parangtritis Geomaritime Science Park

    Dive into the cultural and scientific wonders at the Parangtritis Geomaritime Science Park. Here, you can explore exhibits that unravel the mysteries of maritime science and the rich history of the Javanese coast. It’s a place where learning and adventure go hand in hand.

  • 5. The Thrill of Gumuk Pasir Parangkusumo

    For the adrenaline junkies, Gumuk Pasir Parangkusumo is the place to be. This sand dune offers the perfect terrain for thrilling sandboarding adventures. Experience the rush as you navigate the undulating dunes, making memories that will last a lifetime.

  • 6. Barchan Sand Dunes

    Experience the thrill of adventure at Gumuk Pasir Barchan, where the golden dunes stretch as far as the eye can see. These natural sand dunes are perfect for sandboarding, offering a unique and exhilarating way to explore this extraordinary landscape. The shifting sands and the breathtaking views make it a must-visit destination for thrill-seekers and nature lovers alike.

  • 7. The Serene Laguna Depok

    Discover the serene beauty of Laguna Depok, a picturesque lagoon that invites you to unwind and reconnect with nature. Take a leisurely boat ride, enjoy bird watching, or simply relax by the water’s edge, surrounded by lush greenery and tranquil waters.

  • 8. The Sacred Aura of Parangkusumo

    Feel the spiritual energy at Parangkusumo Beach, known for its sacred rituals and ceremonies. Join the locals in their traditional offerings, witness the unique cultural practices, and embrace the serene atmosphere that pervades this sacred space.

  • 9. The Timeless Charm of Cemara Sewu

    Cemara Sewu Beach offers a unique charm with its dense casuarina groves and peaceful ambiance. Stroll through the shaded pathways, enjoy a picnic under the trees, and let the soothing sounds of the ocean calm your mind.

  • 10. Parangwedang

    Relax and rejuvenate at Parangwedang, a natural hot spring known for its therapeutic properties. Nestled amidst lush greenery, this tranquil spot provides the perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life. Soak in the warm, mineral-rich waters and let the natural surroundings soothe your mind and body.

  • 11. Turtle Breeding

    At Penangkaran Penyu (Turtle Conservation), witness the incredible journey of sea turtles from eggs to adulthood. This conservation site is dedicated to protecting these magnificent creatures, offering educational tours and hands-on experiences. Learn about the vital conservation efforts and maybe even have the chance to release baby turtles into the ocean.

  • 12. Petilasan Ngrawa Aji

    Step into the past at Petilasan Ngrawa Aji, a site steeped in history and legend. This sacred spot is believed to be the meditation place of the ancient king, Ngrawa Aji. The serene environment and the mystical ambiance invite you to reflect and connect with the rich cultural heritage of the region.

  • 13. Petilasan Maulana Magribi dan Syekh Bela Belu

    Discover the spiritual significance of Petilasan Maulana Magribi dan Syekh Bela Belu. These ancient pilgrimage sites honor two revered Islamic figures, offering a place of reflection and devotion. The peaceful atmosphere and the historical importance of these sites provide a deep sense of connection to the spiritual heritage of the region.

  • 14. Fish auction place

    Imagine the vibrant hustle and bustle of the Tempat Pelelangan Ikan (Fish Auction Place). Here, the air is filled with the lively banter of local fishermen and the aroma of fresh seafood. As the sun rises, the market comes alive with activity, offering visitors a unique glimpse into the daily lives of the fishermen and a chance to savor the freshest catches of the day.

Exploring these amazing places on the coast of Parangtritis Beach offers a diverse and enriching experience. From historical and spiritual sites to thrilling adventures and serene escapes, each destination tells a unique story, inviting you to immerse yourself in the rich tapestry of culture, nature, and history.
